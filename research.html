Atomic Structure of Xenon

AMO Physics Research Group · CU Denver · Present

Modeling the atomic structure of xenon using the Dirac-Hartree-Fock method — a computational approach to the relativistic many-electron problem. The goal is to describe how electrons behave in a heavy atom where relativistic effects can't be ignored.

Presentations

Science as a Mechanism for Cultural Evolution

Western Regional Honors Conference · 2025

Exploring how scientific ideas have shaped what we understand as "truth," and the role science plays in cultural evolution more broadly.

Broader Interests

I'm drawn to theoretical physics — the kind of work that sits at the boundary between physics and philosophy. Questions about the nature of spacetime, the structure of reality at fundamental scales, and how mathematics connects to the physical world. I'm still early in building the foundation for that kind of work, but it's where I want to end up.
